Zakat Awareness

The Importance of Zakat

Zakat is a mandatory charitable contribution, often considered a tax. It is the third pillar of Islam and serves to purify one's wealth and character by sharing with those in need.

Please contact our administrative office for information on how to calculate your Zakat obligations according to Shari'ah principles.
